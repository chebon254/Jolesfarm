About Our Farm

Farming With Love - Quality Food for a Growing Urban Population

We are based in Athi River, Machakos County and in Kibos, Kisumu County. By leveraging conservation and best agricultural practices we seek to produce high-quality food to feed a growing urban population.

How your food is grown or raised can have a major impact on your health as well as the environment. We avoid all artificial chemicals, antibiotics or genetically modified organisms to ensure you get fresh and cheap organic products delivered to your home or office on the same or the next day.
